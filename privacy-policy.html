Privacy Policy

Updated June 12, 2023

At IMDANIELAUSTIN | ASTNDSGN, we are committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of our customers. We understand the importance of maintaining the confidentiality of your data and are dedicated to ensuring that all first-party customer data is private and protected.
We do not sell or exchange any first-party customer data with third-party entities. All data collected by us is solely used to improve our services, tailor your experience, and communicate with you regarding updates and promotions.

We also comply with all applicable laws and regulations governing data privacy, including the EU's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).
We value the trust our customers have placed in us, and we will continue to prioritize the security and privacy of your data. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please do not hesitate to contact us.
